EZ B.O.M.

Rainbow Design Services, Inc. has been providing a multitude of services to the Cable/MSO, Utility, Telco and generally to anyone building and maintaining communications infrastructure for many years. Rainbow specializes in Engineering and Documentation of these networks – and in doing so has helped provide, used for quality assurance purposes, as well as for reporting incremental work processes – the B.O.M. functions in Engineering solutions like Bentley Comms (Focus 95/98/One).

During most of that time, Rainbow project managers and production teams have heard from many customers that better reporting, more detailed reports and easier access to the data warehoused in the Engineering database (Oracle) is in great demand. These same needs and for many of the same reasons since we are a production environment – precipitated the development of a solution: EZ B.O.M. ™

EZ B.O.M. ™ is a 100% web-based, cross-browser compliant set of Reporting Tools integrated with Engineering and GIS platforms like Bentley’s Comms V8.5 products. As new versions of these platforms are adopted, we will be quick to extend the functionality of EZ B.O.M. ™ to those new platforms and adapt to any potential changes in data model. The first version of this product is fully integrated with Bentley Comms V8 and soon with Bentley Comms V8i, and in early 2011 (1st Quarter) will be with the SPATIALnet platform from SPATIALinfo. All of these platforms leverage Oracle 8, 9i, 10g or 11g.

EZ B.O.M. ™ provides the following general functionality by default: (Example here is for RF/Design Module reporting)

  • Activities by Project
  • Cable by Project
  • Taps by Project
  • 2 Ways by Project
  • 3 Ways by Project
  • Power Supplies by Project
  • CBS by Project
  • List Nodes
  • List Maps
  • Full Report
  • Reports are fully sort-able (on the fly)
  • Reports can be exported to Excel with one click (for custom formatting etc)

Much more customization and functionality is in the works – and we are constantly seeking new ideas, so if you don’t see something you want out of a web-based reporting tool – just ask! We will make it appear.

Our goal with EZ B.O.M.™ is to provide our customers with an INSTANT answer to the reporting needs around their Engineering and Plant data. No more is it acceptable to rely on the software vendors to provide reporting tools and interfaces, they just haven’t had the time to focus on metrics while building Engineering environments – but we have.

EZ B.O.M.™ is 100% web-based and cross-browser functional means that these reports can be accessed from anywhere, and with any device (even mobile). We’ve tested with I-PHONES and IPADs too, this reporting engine works with those devices and means that even when you are in that important meeting and are asked that question that historically it might have taken hours or days to provide --- you can get instant details in a few moments right there in that meeting!!

The data EZ B.O.M.™ provides is LIVE too, as live as your Engineering Server is. The data is driven directly by connection to Oracle, so even that extension and new design done yesterday or as recent as this morning – if it was posted to Oracle – the metrics are in your report!

EZ B.O.M.™ caches your reports too – so over time you can see progressive changes, trends, and use the reports to do time-based analysis. Run the reports regularly or on a schedule and you’ll have great snapshots like never before into what’s truly transpiring with your Engineering data and where time/effort/money is being spent.

Are you storing COST metrics in your Engineering database? Many of our customers haven’t taken full advantage of those cost fields in the Equipment profiles – perhaps it’s because the reporting interface wasn’t strong enough to justify entering that data. Maybe now is the time?
