Who We Are

Rainbow Design Services is a leading provider of NETWORK ENGINEERING, FIELD SERVICES and GIS/MAPPING SERVICES including documentation, audits, surveys and software development.

The primary mission of Rainbow Design Services, Inc. is to provide our customers with a product and service that are consistently equal to or better than the high standards we set for ourselves, while ensuring that our employees are constantly motivated and trained to meet the ever changing needs in our business.
